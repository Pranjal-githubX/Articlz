  1. Money
    1. 50:30:20 RULE:

      2. It tells us how much money of our income should be spent on what type of expenses:

      • Needs: 50% - Essentials : food, rent, bills
      • Wants: 30% - Desires - New Gadget, Vaccation
      • Savings: 20% - Investments, Debt repayments.
    2. Automate Your Investments

      3. After getting your pay check, first transfer the money for your essential expenses, Then use it for your own things

    3. Try to find new income-generating instruments for investing.
      • Haven't tried MFs? Give it a try.
      • Haven't tried FDs or Fixed-Interest yielding investments? Give it a try.

  2. Career
    1. Change Your aspect of current job

      2. It can be changing your domain or changing your current company.

      For The SAKE OF Good Income

    2. Take Feedback on your performance from at least 3 people

      3. The person you are asking can be an inferior or your superior. Do it every month

    3. Learn in-demand skills every 6 months

      4. For you to be aware of latest trends and to survive in the AI era

      A Research says: You get under 20% of people if you have done 20 hours on a specific topic

    4. Start a side hustle

      5. You can start doing work on a skill you are good at.

      Professionaly speaking, do some freelance work for side income

    5. Attend at least 2 conferences or networking events in your domain in 2025
  3. Health
    1. Set a health goal that you have not done in life ever

      2. Start a new Health Routine. Like:

      • Drinking Milk every day
      • Consuming less sugar
      • Drinking less tea etc.
    2. Do meditation every day

      3. People who have done meditation have witness a tremendous change in their life.

      Start small - 5 mins daily. But be CONSISTENT

    3. Fix Your Sleep Pattern

      4. Sleep and Wake up at a fixed time. Always

      It can be difficult in starting phase, but will be easier in the long-term

    4. Start playing a new sport.

      5. You should be playing a sport in your life.
      You can play once in a week, But again - BE CONSISTENT

    5. Get a comprehensive check-up for your body

      6. It is very necessary. No matter if you're in your 20s, 30s or 50s
